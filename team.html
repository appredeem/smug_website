Management Team
Sheffield Nolan - Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Prior to Smug, Sheffield founded AppTrailers, driving new users for 90% of the App Store's 100 Top Grossing developers including TinyCo, Kabam, GREE, Ngmoco, Zynga, Funzio, Gameloft, CrowdStar, Disney, Priceline, Smule, AOL, Addmired, Breaktime Studios and many others. Before that Sheffield developed apps that climbed to the top 5 paid and free positions in the Apple App Store (U.S. and international markets). Sheffield has also architected and managed large scale solutions for many Fortune 500 companies and venture backed startups, including Visa, eBay, PayPal, AltaVista and Tapulous. Sheffield is passionate about creating and building early stage disruptive companies and pivoting in reaction to changing market and competitive conditions.

Jordan McColloch – Chief Operating Officer

Prior to Smug, Jordan was an early employee at eHealth, Inc. and led customer acquisition through online and offline advertising. He most recently served as Vice President of Marketing. Before that Jordan held several marketing management positions with Colgate-Palmolive. Jordan has a B.S. from the University of Southern California and an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management.

James Ransom - Vice President, Engineering

Prior to Smug, James was the 3rd employee at Crowdstar. As a lead engineer and producer, James launched many Facebook and iOS applications including Happy Pets (Facebook), Modern Girl (iOS) and It Girl (Facebook). James has over 10 years of experience in the social and mobile space and has a B.S. in computer science from the University of California, Davis.

Frank Mattox – Chief Revenue Officer Frank has over 20 years of experience in managing Sales and Marketing teams with a focus on the Internet and mobile marketplaces. Frank has launched several mobile applications and promotional website services, and managed numerous Fortune 500 infrastructure projects.
