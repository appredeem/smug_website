Smug. Free messaging done right.

Smug is the messenger available for iPhone, iPod and iPad and all Android devices. Smug uses your 3G/4G or WiFi to message with your friends and family. Switch from SMS to Smug to send and receive unlimited text messages, photos, voice messages, and video messages. With Smug, your friends on Android can message you on iPhone, iPod and iPad and you can message them!

You can also earn Amazon gift cards for inviting friends!

An HTML version of Smug Messenger is also available for your web browser.
